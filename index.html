Welcome to Anuj Children's Hospital

Anuj Children's Hospital is exclusively dedicated to pediatric care.It was incepted in the year 1994. They are one of the well-known Hospitals in Varasia. Backed with a vision to offer the best in patient care and equipped with technologically advanced healthcare facilities, they are one of the upcoming names in the healthcare industry. Located in Varasia, this hospital is easily accessible by various means of transport. A team of well-trained medical staff, non-medical staff and experienced clinical technicians work round-the-clock to offer various services . Their professional services make them a sought after Hospitals in Vadodara. A team of doctors on board, including specialists are equipped with the knowledge and expertise for handling various types of medical cases.
